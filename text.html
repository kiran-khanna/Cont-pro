FOUNDATION:


Pile foundation as per the structural design and Individual RCC Column erected on top of the pile capThoroughly consolidated with water and Ramped well additionally Anti -Termite treatment will be provided

STRUCTURE:


RCC Framed structures with column beams and slabs.All columns are erected as per the specifications of the Structural Engineer using ISI marked TMT steel rods.

WALLS:


All outer walls are 9'thick brick work with Cement mortar and outer opening with Sunshade. All inner walls are 4 1/2 "thick Brickwork with cement mortar.

DOORS:


Main door frame is made of teak wood fitted with good quality flush door with teak finish. Other doors and frames are of country wood with Hydraulic compressed flush skin doors.

WINDOWS:


UPVC windows open type fitted glass.And UPVC ventilators with suitable louvered glass panels and Iron grills.

FLOORING:


Entire area by vitrified 2x2 ceramic Floor Tiles,skirting 4 inches from Floor. Antiskid ceramic tiles flooring for staircase and landing area.

KITCHEN:


Granite Platform provided with stainless steel Sink with drain board. One cupboard and one loft, glazed tiles upto 4 Feet from platform.

BED ROOM:


One open Cupboard and one loft will be provided.

WASH BASIN:


One Wash Basin for each residential flat.

BATHROOM & TOILET


Bathrooms will be provided with wall mixture and a shower Geyser point will be provided in one bathrooms. In wall up to 7feet height dado tiles and anti skid tiles for flooring. Sanitary & all CP fittings will be of parryware make or equivalent.

PLUMBING :


ISi mark CPVC concealed plumbing lines for hot and cold water and PVC plumbing lines for outer.

WATER:


Bore well for water supply connected to over head tank, separate sump and over-head tank for Corporation water supply to kitchens only.

ELECTRICAL:


Modular Switches, 3Phase Wiring,Inverter Point, Branded Fire Resistant Cable Of ISI Make, Manuval Phase Changer.

PAINTING:


Internal walls will bo finished with 1 coat of primer, 2 coats of putty & 2 coats of interior emulsion. Celling with 1 coat white cement and 2 coats of ace emulsion, exterior walls finished with 1 coat white cement and 2 coats of exterior emulsion. Grills and wood works finished with 1 coat primer and 2 coats of enamel paint.

SUMP/SEP'IICTANK:


13,000 Liters Capacity tank of 41/2 Brickwork and 41/2 RCC wall and finished with RCC slab on top.

PEST CONTROL:


Anti-termite treatment at different stages.

RAIN WATER:


Rain water harvesting will be provided.
